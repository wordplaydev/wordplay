Make characters grow with sound

Did you know your project can listen to you? The @Volume @Stream uses your microphone to give us a number from \0\ (silent) to \1\ (loud).

We can use that number to control anything — the size of a @Phrase, its rotation, its color.

\
Phrase('📣' size: (Volume() × 5m) + 1m)
\

When it's quiet, the megaphone is \1m\ tall. When you shout, it can grow up to \6m\ tall.

Want a sun that spins faster the louder you are?

\
Phrase('🌞' rotation: Volume() × 360°)
\

Try clapping or humming and watch what happens.
(Your computer may ask permission to use the microphone the first time.)
