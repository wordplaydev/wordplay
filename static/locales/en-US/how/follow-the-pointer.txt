Make a character follow your finger

Want a character that chases your mouse or finger around the @Stage?
We can do that with the @Pointer input.

@Pointer gives us a fresh @Place every time the pointer moves.
If we use that @Place as a @Phrase's \place\, the character will go wherever we point.

\
Stage([Phrase('🐈' place: Pointer())] place: Place(0m 0m -10m))
\

That's it! Move your mouse or drag your finger, and watch the cat follow.

You can do more interesting things by pulling out the \x\ and \y\ from the @Place.
Here's a duck that's always floating a meter above the pointer:

\
Stage([Phrase('🦆' place: Place(Pointer().x Pointer().y + 1m))] place: Place(0m 0m -10m))
\
