Put text side by side

Sometimes we want text to sit next to each other in a tidy line.
We can do that with a @Row, which arranges everything in a @Group from left to right.

\
Group(Row() [
    Phrase('🍎')
    Phrase('🍌')
    Phrase('🍇')
])
\

A @Row can also line characters up by their top, middle, or bottom edges, and add space between them.
Here is the same fruit, but bottom-aligned with a little breathing room:

\
Group(Row('>' 0.5m) [
    Phrase('🍎' size: 2m)
    Phrase('🍌' size: 1m)
    Phrase('🍇' size: 3m)
])
\

The \'>'\ tells the @Row to line everything up along its bottom edge. Try \'<'\ for top or \'|'\ for middle.
The \0.5m\ is the space between each character.
