Pick a random thing from a list

Want your project to surprise you each time it runs? Make a list of choices and pick one with @Random.

@Random can give us a random number, and a list can be looked up by number with \[n]\.
Put them together to pick a random emoji:

\
emojis: ['🌈' '🌟' '🎈' '🦄' '🍩']
Phrase(emojis.random())
\

You can also use @Random with two numbers, which picks a whole number between them.
For example, to pick a /new/ random emoji every second, mix in @Time and a @Reaction:

\
emojis: ['🌈' '🌟' '🎈' '🦄' '🍩']
pick: emojis[1] … ∆ Time(1000ms) … emojis[Random(1 emojis.length())]
Phrase(pick)
\

Each tick of @Time, the @Reaction picks a new surprise.
