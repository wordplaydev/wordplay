Stack characters top to bottom

If a @Row is for side by side, then a @Stack is for top to bottom.
@Stack puts each character below the one before it, like a tower.

\
Group(Stack() [
    Phrase('one')
    Phrase('two')
    Phrase('three')
])
\

Like a @Row, a @Stack can also have an alignment and padding.
Try lining everything up along the left side:

\
Group(Stack('<' 0.25m) [
    Phrase('🐣 little')
    Phrase('🐥 medium')
    Phrase('🐔 big')
])
\

The \'<'\ means /line everything up along the left edge/.
You can also try \'|'\ for center or \'>'\ for the right edge.
